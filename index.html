Let's play Blackjack!

How to play

Your objective: score as close to 21 as possible and beat the dealer's score

You lose if you go bust (score more than 21) or the dealer scores higher than you without going bust

Click on the card deck to be dealt your first two cards

If you think you need to add another card to get closer to 21, hit 'Twist' otherwise hit 'Stick'

Aces count as 11 unless your current score is more than 21 in which case they count as 1

If the dealer's current score is under 16 they will always add another card

Click 'Finish' to see the final result

Let's go!
